Norbert

A simple & lightweight jQuery plugin for placeholders

This is Norbert

Yes, that pale, semi-bald guy there...
Norbert is a simple UI/UX assistant (and a jQuery plugin) that will help you by giving you hints at what was in your placeholders fields, before you filled them.

What is Norbert made of ?

Let's put our X-Rays goggles on, and see what's inside Norbert.
And just how he is built...

This is his heart

A big good ol' heart to display like that :

          
              <script type="text/javascript" src="http://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/
1.7.2/jquery.min.js">
            
            </script>

...and that you can kickstart with that simple formulae :

          
            <script>
            

             $(document).ready(function() {
            

              $('#yourForm').norbert({
            

               position: 'left' // Position can be 'top', 'left', 'bottom' or 'right'
            

            });
          

           });
          

          </script>

Let's see his brain, now

These two things are what allows Norbert to make his magic :

          
              <script type="text/javascript" src="norbert.js"></script>
            

            <link rel="stylesheet" href="norbert.css">

And so, here's his skull

Required to have Norbert, well, hum, just stand up :

          
            <form action="" id="yourForm">
              

               <label for="name">
                

                  <span>Name</span>
                

                  <input type="text" placeholder="Enter your name" id="name"class="norbert">
                

                 </label>
                

            </form>

Let's drive,
Norbert !
